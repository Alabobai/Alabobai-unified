# AI Executive Team Playbook

## Mission
Build Alabobai into the default AI operating system for non-technical founders by running a dual-engine company:
1. **Growth Engine** (brand, demand, revenue, partnerships)
2. **Protection Engine** (finance, legal, risk, reliability, wealth protection)

---

## 1) Executive AI Team (Role Charter)

### 1. CEO Chief of Staff AI (Command Center)
**Owns:** priorities, cadence, decisions, cross-team alignment  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- Monday executive brief (scorecard + risks + priorities)
- Friday retrospective (wins, misses, corrections)
- Decision log updates

### 2. CMO AI (Brand + Demand)
**Owns:** positioning, content engine, campaigns, funnel performance  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- 3 authority content pieces (founder POV)
- 1 flagship campaign launch/iteration
- Funnel report (traffic → lead → meeting)

### 3. CRO AI (Revenue + Sales Ops)
**Owns:** pipeline, close rates, enterprise outreach, partner conversion  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- Pipeline health snapshot
- 20 strategic outbound touches
- Deal obstacle report + next-step plan per top deal

### 4. Partnerships & Enterprise AI
**Owns:** big-company feature opportunities, co-marketing, pilots  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- Target account list updates
- 5 partner intros/proposals
- Pilot-to-contract conversion plan

### 5. CFO/Treasury AI
**Owns:** cashflow, margin, budget, capital allocation, runway  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- 13-week cashflow model update
- Spend efficiency report
- Credit utilization report (personal + business)

### 6. Grants & Non-Dilutive Capital AI
**Owns:** grant sourcing, applications, deadlines, submission quality  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- 5 grant opportunities qualified
- 2 application drafts/submissions
- Funding probability dashboard

### 7. Legal & Wealth Protection AI
**Owns:** structure prep, trust strategy prep, compliance docs (with counsel)  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- Entity/ownership map updates
- Risk register updates
- Legal packet drafts for attorney/CPA review

### 8. CPO/CTO AI
**Owns:** product roadmap, reliability, launch gates, observability  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- Milestone progress report
- Incident + SLO summary
- Release readiness checklist

### 9. Customer Success & Research AI
**Owns:** voice of customer, onboarding, retention drivers  
**Weekly deliverables:**
- Top customer pain/feature insight summary
- Activation-to-outcome report
- Churn risk list + save actions

---

## 2) Weekly Operating System (Exact Cadence)

## Monday — Executive Command Day
- Review full KPI scorecard
- Set top 3 priorities for the week
- Approve resource allocation
- Publish weekly directive

## Tuesday — Product + Reliability Day
- Roadmap checkpoint
- Reliability/SLO/incident review
- Security/compliance posture review

## Wednesday — Brand + Growth Day
- Content publishing cadence
- Campaign performance + creative iteration
- Funnel bottleneck removal

## Thursday — Revenue + Capital Day
- Enterprise deal reviews
- Partnerships and pilots
- Grant pipeline + submission progress

## Friday — Finance + Legal + Risk Day
- Cashflow and margin review
- Credit optimization actions
- Legal/trust/tax prep review with pending counsel actions
- Weekly retro + next week setup

---

## 3) KPI Scorecard (Run Every Monday)

## North Star
- **% of users reaching first business outcome in 7 days**

## Growth KPIs
- Qualified leads/week
- Activation rate
- CAC and payback period
- Content-attributed pipeline

## Revenue KPIs
- MRR/ARR growth
- Pipeline value and velocity
- Win rate
- Expansion revenue

## Product KPIs
- Time-to-first-outcome
- Weekly retained users
- Feature adoption for flagship workflows
- Error rates on critical paths

## Reliability KPIs
- Uptime/SLO attainment
- P95 latency
- Incident count + MTTR
- Release rollback frequency

## Capital & Finance KPIs
- Runway (months)
- Gross margin
- Cash conversion cycle
- Grant submissions/wins

## Credit KPIs
- Personal utilization ratio
- Business utilization ratio
- On-time payment rate
- Available credit growth

## Legal/Protection KPIs
- Compliance milestones completed
- Risk register severity trend
- Contract turnaround time
- Counsel-reviewed structure milestones

---

## 4) 90-Day Execution Map

## Days 1–30 (Foundation)
- Finalize brand strategy + ICP wedge
- Launch content engine and CRM funnel
- Implement executive KPI dashboard
- Build grant and credit baseline trackers
- Define legal structure options with counsel prep docs
- Ship and validate flagship workflow #1

## Days 31–60 (Acceleration)
- Increase outbound and partnerships
- Submit first grant batch
- Improve conversion and onboarding
- Strengthen reliability and enterprise trust assets
- Expand product moat in core workflow depth

## Days 61–90 (Conversion)
- Convert pilots to paid contracts
- Scale distribution channels with proven CAC
- Finalize legal/wealth protection actions with licensed advisors
- Publish proof artifacts (case studies, results, reliability)
- Set next 2-quarter expansion plan

---

## 5) Decision Rules (Non-Negotiable)
1. No major feature ships without telemetry and rollback path.
2. No campaign scales without measurable conversion and payback logic.
3. No legal/wealth implementation without licensed professional review.
4. 80% of execution goes to the chosen wedge ICP.
5. Every week ends with a written decision log.
